## How to make a SBB Coil?

Its simple to make a SBB Coil (Saint Buster Button) or a Galaxy Coil.

## You need for that:

– Copper wire minimum 1 mm2 cross section or more, it can be enamelled wire or bare copper wire, minimum 60cm long.
– Anything round like a nail or a metal drill, about 3 - 5mm diameter.
Bend the wire in the middle but so, it has still a round bend.
Bend the wire at the bend around the nail like on the photo and cut the ends, so that both ends are same long.

Bend the wire lug up like on the photo (most possible by hand, otherwise by pliers).
Form it by hand to a Galaxy Coil.
Bend so long by hand till the spiral is right. Finish.
